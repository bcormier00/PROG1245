Biography

Wayne Gretzy is a former ice hockey player from Canada who still holds many boastful records to this day. He spent most of his career playing for the Edmonton Oilers and the Los Angeles Kings. He is known as the best NHL player to ever live. Therefore he has been coined with the name "The Great One."
